Movies Tierlist: Goated Edition

S-tier

Spider-man: Into the spiderverse

Undoubtedly THE greates movie of all time. It has everything; impeccable buildup, great characters and most importantly, unforgettable villains. Miles has just been bitten by a radioactive spider and is struggling to come to terms with his new powers.

Inception

This came a close second but it is must watch for everyone. It is jam packed with action while also hitting hard with philosophical ideologies. Dom Cobb is dream hopping and is fighting baddies

The Batman

Taking place in the gritty streets of Gotham, yet another reboot of Batman is born. This one is more grisly and darker than the rest and will leave you at the edge of your seat
